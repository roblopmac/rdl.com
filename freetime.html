Main Hobby : Photography

When I am not developing or learning software, I am probably outside taking photos, chasing a sunrise or a sunset, or perhaps preparing the photo equipment for a long night shooting the stars.

Stars Trail - backyard experiment by Ruben Dario Lopez on 500px

That is my hobby and it is important to me. It helps to release the stress, helps to explorer my creativity and it has multiple areas to explore. For instance, all photos used in this website were shooting by me, some of those were shooting time ago.

Adelaide River Torrens Futuristic Footpath - under by Ruben Dario Lopez on 500px

A hobby like photography, or any other kind of hobby, is a point of connection with more people, increases different areas of your brain, and helps to discover new horizons. It perhaps can make you change the perspective of some things.

I also have a couple of drones that I like to fly and use to take videos and photos. Those were built by me, as I think that the best way to learn something is by knowing what is ‘under the hood’, and by reading, buying the components, soldering and ensemble everything I learned a lot about how these things can fly, what are their limits and danger. I wouldn’t say that these drones or multi-copters are toys. Definitely DRONES are not toys.
